Choose a Range !!!

Instructions:

Enter a range and we will generate a magic number.

You will have to guess the magic number in order to win the game

You will get score based on the number of attempts you took to guess correctly

You will have a relief or buffer range.

If you guess a number within the buffer your guess is considered as correct.

For every 100 gap you will have a buffer of 5.

If the difference between maximum and minimum value is less than 100 then buffer is 2.
